About Valhalla Ink

Valhalla Tattoos In the Heart of Galway City, Is a small tight knit tattoo parlour, Valhalla Ink is known for their Celtic and Norse Mythology designs. With our two in House tattoo artist with 15+ years experience between them, Valhalla Ink can make your creative ideas come to life.

Situated Slap bang in the middle of Spanish Arch, Galway, Valhalla Ink is surrounded by some of the most Historically rich areas in the County. With structures dating back to the Vikings era themselves. Some say the longships would have beached at the harbour at the shop's very doorstep.
